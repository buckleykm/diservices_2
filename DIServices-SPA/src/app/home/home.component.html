  • Long-term care insurance helps pay the expense of long-term care. It can help pay for home health care, assisted living facility care and more. Most importantly it helps seniors protect their assets, maintain control and alleviate family members and friends from the burdens of care giving.

  • Disability insurance is also known as paycheck protection or income protection. These products provide replacement income when a breadwinner is unable to work due to illness or injury. Disability insurance is essential coverage for business owners and high-earning professionals.
